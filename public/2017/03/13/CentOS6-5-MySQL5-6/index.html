需要下载的三个安装包：

  • MySQL-client-5.6.35-1.el6.x86_64.rpm
  • MySQL-devel-5.6.35-1.el6.x86_64.rpm
  • MySQL-server-5.6.35-1.el6.x86_64.rpm
